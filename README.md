## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Computer Engineering student and Research Assistant** at **Paderborn University**, working in the **EIM-I department** on the intersection of **Approximate Computing** and **Machine Learning**. My research focuses on making neural networks faster, smaller, and more efficient — without sacrificing meaningful accuracy.

I have hands-on experience with **HPC clusters** (Noctua 2 @ PC2), building ML pipelines, and implementing everything from low-level hardware approximations to high-level LLM fine-tuning workflows.

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Approximate Computing** — hardware-software co-design for efficient inference
- **Neural Network Compression** — pruning, quantization, SVD-based decomposition
- **Reservoir Computing & RNNs** — Liquid State Machines, sparse connectivity
- **LLM Optimization** — fine-tuning, RAG pipelines, and agent-based architectures
